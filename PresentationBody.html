Alright, so the interview day is finally here.

Your mind is probably going crazy with questions: how do I present yourself? What do I say? Do not worry - Bridge to Your Success is here to help!

  • What to Say When You Arrive

    • You should introduce yourself to the receptionist by stating your name and purpose. Do not sound too excited as he/she may get annoyed.

  • Know your "Brand"
     

    • Ask yourself: What is your purpose, missions, and values?

  • Keep your Introduction Precise!

    • Focus on the key elements in your background that sets you apart from others. Point out your interests, skills, experience, and personal qualities that will help you excel at the job.

  • What to Say When You Meet the Hiring Manager

    • Offer to shake hands first - it will make you seem more poised. Take time to introduce yourself and tell the interviewer that it is a pleasure to meet them. Remember: smile and make eye contact!

  • Know your Audience
     

    • Know your interviewer's style and culture and appeal to their interests. It will make them want to listen to you more.

  • Be a Storyteller
     

    • Present your attributes articulately. Be animated, enthusiastic, and authentic, but do not overdo it.

  • Show, Do Not Tell
     

    • Use examples to illustrate your traits rather than telling your interviewer. Prove your successes to the interviewer. This will keep them interested.

  • Plan to Articulate How You Meet the Employer's Needs

    • Be sure to review the necessary qualifications and the job's duties. Identify the skills necessary to complete the job successfully. Tell the interviewer any examples that prove your skills and qualifications for the job.

Tips for a Successful Interview

Bridge to Your Success has some tips to help you out on your interview day. Just follow these tips and you will be set!

  • Research the Organization
     

    • Try to ask yourself these questions before your interview: What is their mission? How is it unique compared to other companies with the same purpose? How can I appeal to the company?

  • Know How To Answer Common Interview Questions
     

    • Practice makes perfect! Know how to answer the following: Tell me about yourself. Why do you want to work here? Why do you want this position? What makes you fit for this job? What are your strengths and weaknesses?

  • Be Authentic and Professional
     

    • Be sure to maintain eye contact, but do not stare at them for too long to make them uncomfortable! Do not be too tense and nervous. Bring a water bottle to freshen up. Show your interviewer your personality and humor; it is absolutely okay to make them laugh!

  • DOs
    • Arrive a few minutes early
    • Remember the names of the hiring team
    • Ask for clarification if you don't understand a question
    • Pause if you need time to think
    • Send a thank-you note or email afterwards that addresses specific issues you discussed during the interview
  • DON'Ts
    • Assume the hiring team remembers your résumé
    • Talk constantly
    • Discuss salary and financial benefits
    • Beg for the job
    • Wear strong scents
    • Dress or behave too casually
    • Ladies:
      • No plunging, deep v-neck
      • Avoid looking too old-fashioned
      • Don't overdo make-up
      • Accessories are minimal
    • No extremely bright colors
    • Have sweaty palms
